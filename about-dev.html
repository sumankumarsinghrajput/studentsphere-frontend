Student Sphere Team

Group Project · B.Tech Final Year

Student Sphere is an academic management system developed by a team of B.Tech students as part of a final-year project. It is designed to manage students, faculty, attendance, marks, and study resources efficiently using a clean and responsive interface built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
