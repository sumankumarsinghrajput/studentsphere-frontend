Features

Everything You Need,
In One Place

From attendance tracking to file sharing, Student Sphere covers every academic need for students, faculty, and administrators.

📅
Attendance Tracking
Faculty update attendance per semester. Students see their live percentage with eligibility status and exam access.
📊
Marks & Grades
View percentage scores with automatic grade cards (A–F). Includes performance tags and pass/fail indicators.
📓
Study Notes
Faculty upload PDF notes per semester. Students download them directly from their dashboard anytime.
📝
Assignments
Faculty share assignment files for each class. Students can view and download submissions from one place.
🔬
Lab Reports
Organize and distribute lab work digitally. Students get instant access to all their lab materials.
🛡️
Admin Approval System
Every new registration requires admin approval before access is granted — keeping the platform secure and organized.
⬆️
Semester Promotion
Admins can bulk-promote entire semesters with one click — with an undo option for safety.
🌗
Dark & Light Mode
Fully themed UI with smooth dark/light mode toggle. Your preference is saved across sessions.
📱
Mobile App (APK)

How It Works

Up and Running
in Minutes

Getting started with Student Sphere is simple. Four steps and your entire institution is connected.

1
Register Your Account
Sign up with your name, email, role (student or faculty), and semester. The form takes under a minute.
2
Wait for Approval
Your registration is reviewed by the admin. You'll be able to log in as soon as approval is granted.
3
Log In & Explore
Once approved, sign in to access your personalized dashboard — tailored to your role.
4
Stay On Top
Track attendance, check marks, download notes, and stay up to date with all academic activity.
