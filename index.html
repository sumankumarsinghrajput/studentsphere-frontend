Features

Everything You Need, In One Place

From attendance tracking to file sharing, Student Sphere covers every academic need.

📅
Attendance Tracking
Faculty update attendance per semester. Students see live percentage and exam eligibility.
📊
Marks & Grades
Automatic grade cards (A–F) with performance tags and pass/fail indicators.
📓
Study Notes
Faculty upload PDF notes. Students view inside the app and download anytime.
📝
Assignments
Faculty share assignments with due dates. Students submit work directly from the dashboard.
🔬
Lab Reports
Distribute and submit lab work digitally — no paper needed.
📢
Notice Board
Faculty post announcements targeted at specific semesters or the entire institution.
🛡️
Admin Approvals
Every registration requires admin approval — keeping the platform secure and organized.
⬆️
Semester Promotion
Admins bulk-promote entire semesters in one click, with undo support.
🌑
Dark & Light Mode
Smooth theme toggle saved across sessions. Looks great in both modes.
